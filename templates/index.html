Track your Elected Officials, Vote with Facts

In a democracy, where we have the power to elect officials, we should have access to information about our leaders in an efficient, accessible and easy way. Introducing: our dashboards. Leaders make promises on what they plan to fulfill during their term. It is hard for the public to keep track of what promises they made and how much they have met, especially when the promises were made years ago. In this way, we hope to achieve two goals - keeping our leaders accountable and making informed decisions for re-elections. Voting is important. Making progress on a government level is important. Let us use our right to continue improving America.

Why is it important to vote with facts?

An important responsibility of citizens is voting. The law does not require citizens to vote, but voting is a very important part of any democracy. By voting, citizens are participating in the democratic process. Citizens vote for leaders to represent them and their ideas, and the leaders support the citizens' interests.
